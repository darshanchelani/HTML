4. Common Video and Audio Formats

Format Description
MP4 (Video) A widely used video format known for its high compression and compatibility.
WebM (Video) An open, royalty-free video format designed for the web.
MP3 (Audio) A popular audio format known for its good compression and widespread support.
OGG (Audio) An open, free audio format that provides good quality and compression.
WAV (Audio) An uncompressed audio format that offers high fidelity but large file sizes.
